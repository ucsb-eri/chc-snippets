Kakuma Refugee Camp Heat Tracker

Extreme heat is a growing health concern particularly for vulnerable communities. The Heat Tracker provides a platform to monitor extreme heat events at the Kakuma refugee camp using the high-resolution CHIRTS-ERA5 temperature dataset and CHIRTS-GEFS temperature forecasts. Below are graphics of the CHIRTS-ERA5 5-day and monthly Maximum Temperature (Tmax) and Heat Index (HI) as well as the CHIRTS-GEFS 5-, 10-, and 15-day temperature forecasts.
