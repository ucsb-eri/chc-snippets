Climate Services Dashboard

The Climate Hazards Center (CHC) supports climate services that strengthen decision-making and resilience across the globe. Below are three key components of CHC’s climate service initiatives.

Early Warning

The CHC advances early warning systems for droughts, floods, and heat hazards by integrating satellite data, ground observations, and model-based forecasts. Through partnerships with various organizations the CHC supports the monitoring and forecasting of extreme climate events.

Capacity Building

CHC works with global and regional partners through training programs and collaborative workshops to strengthen climate monitoring and forecasting capabilities. These initiatives help National Meteorological and Hydrological Centers improve their ability to interpret, communicate, and act on climate information.

Snippets

Snippets are interactive web tools that let users embed near real-time climate maps from the Early Warning eXplorer (EWX) directly into their own websites. These tools allow National Meteorological and Hydrological Services and humanitarian partners to display CHC’s climate visualizations locally, improving access to actionable early warning information.
