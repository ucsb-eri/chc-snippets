Climate Services Dashboard

The Climate Hazards Center (CHC) supports climate services that strengthen decision-making and resilience across the globe. Below are three key components of CHC’s climate service initiatives.

Early Warning

The CHC advances early warning systems for droughts, floods, and heat hazards by integrating satellite data, ground observations, and model-based forecasts. Through partnerships with various organizations the CHC supports the monitoring and forecasting of extreme climate events.

Snippets/h2>

Snippets are a free, publicly accessible tool to display maps from the Climate Hazards Center Early Warning eXplorer (EWX) online application on your own webpage. The CHC maintains extensive data stores that are regularly updated. Snippets allow users embed near real-time climate maps from the EWX directly onto their own websites. These tools allow National Meteorological and Hydrological Services and humanitarian partners to display CHC’s climate visualizations locally, improving access to actionable early warning information.
