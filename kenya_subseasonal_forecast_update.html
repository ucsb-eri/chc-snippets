Kenya Subseasonal Forecast Updates

This page hosts rainfall monitoring and forecast results co-developed by the Kenya Meteorological Department, the Famine Early Warning Systems Network (FEWS NET), the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology, the UCAR Innovative Capacity Development Program, and the Rhiza-led Nimbus program. These results highlight the onset and progression of the rainy season in Kenya, supporting decision-making and early warning efforts related to drought, food security, and flooding.
