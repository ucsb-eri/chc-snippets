Kakuma Refugee Camp Heat Tracker

The Kakuma Heat Tracker monitors extreme heat events in the refugee camp and provides actionable information to reduce heat-related health risks.

Heat Exposure

This section provides detailed maps and charts of daily temperature extremes, heatwaves, and cumulative heat exposure within the Kakuma Refugee Camp. It allows agencies to identify vulnerable areas and times.
